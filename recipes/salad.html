Salad with curd

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Plain yogurt, chilled
  • 1 teaspoon Roasted cumin powder
  • ½ teaspoon Black salt (Kala namak)
  • ¼ teaspoon Black pepper powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • ¼ cup Red onion, chopped
  • ¼ cup Tomato, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Cucumber, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Cilantro or coriander leaves, chopped finely
  • 2 teaspoons Mint leaves, chopped
  • 1 small Green chili, chopped finely

Procedure

  1. Cut all the vegetables
  2. Add all the Ingredients to the vessel and mis it up.
  3. Here the raitha is ready.
