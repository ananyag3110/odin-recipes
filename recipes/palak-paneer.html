Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Palak
  • chillies
  • onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Cashew
  • Masala(Salt, Garam Masala, Kasuri methi, turmeric powder, chilli powder)
  • Whole spices(cardamoms, cumin seeds, cinnamon, cloves)

Procedure

  1. Boil 3 cups water in a pan, microwave or electric heater. Add ¼ teaspoon salt to the hot water and stir.
    Then add the spinach leaves to the hot water. Let the spinach leaves sit in the water for about 1 minute.
  2. Add the spinach in a blender or grinder jar with 1 inch chopped ginger, 1 to 2 garlic cloves and 1 to 2 chili peppers (green chillies).
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons oil, ghee or butter in a pan. Add ½ teaspoon cumin seeds and let them splutter.And then add all spices with onions, wait till onions turns translucent.
  4. Now, add ginger garlic paste and tomatoes.Wait until tomatoes soften.Now add all the masala, cook for a while
  5. Now add the spinach puree that we grinded earlier, add little garam masala. Stir for 2 min with water.
  6. After that add paneer and cook fro 4 min with lid closed.
